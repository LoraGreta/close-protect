Basic Training

Basic licencing is a must in todays' world. You need to have your SIA licence to embark on a close-protection career. Train with one of our security experts in a classroom setting in Hertfordshire, 20 minutes from Kings Cross Train Station in London.

Beyond Basic Training

After obtaining you SIA licence, to be competitive as a close-protection officer you need to have additional experience and be licensed to carry a gun. We have an intake 3 times a year in our facility in Romania which covers firearms training, hand to hand combat, weaponry, evasive car maneuvers on our track and and tailing skills in a city. Our Security Experts are pulled in from around the world as a Tiger Team to best train you, so these individuals are not tachers who "teach all the time". They are working close-protection experts. Topics covered are threat and rsik assessment, operational planning, venue risk, route selection and reconnaisiance, surveillance, and legislation. Firearms training is at a higher level than legally allowed in the UK. Training at our secure facility in Romania.

Survival

Open to the public and only held one time per year. Topics inclide bushcraft, first aid, foraging and wilderness cookery, animal and human tracking, shelters and wilderness navigation. Please contact for further information. Held in Romania.
