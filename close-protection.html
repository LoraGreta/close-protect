Close Protection

Whether it is in your home, whilst you are touring, traveling in areas where of concern, or need a security detail on business, our Close Protection Security Experts (CPSE) are trained to keep you secure. Each contract starts with an Initial Risk Assessment and we are methodical in our assessment of all locations required, individuals to be protected, primary and secondary transportation necessary, stringent contact with outside individuals, and number of CPSE's needed.
