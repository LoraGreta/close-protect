Military

Every member of our team has a military background. We feel a military background is important because it provides discipline and a mental fortitude which we know we can rely on in times which are challenging. In addition, we are proud of the diversity of our men and womens' military background. We have found by blending close protection team members whose military techniques and training are unique based on the country they served, we have built a stronger unit that has gone from strength to strength. Diversity has become our strong point and we are proud to have over 12 different nationalities working together with a single focus: to make the Triad Group the best.

Once Triad Was Born

Triad Group was born when the 3 men, who had been on many successful contracts together over 20 years and in 10 countries, decided to come together to make a powerhouse protection firm. Instead of freelancing they came together because they knew that by uniting their 3 very diverse backgrounds that they would be able to offer the world something unique. They had the proven track record and the contacts. The three men were also not competing with each other: one was a security expert and ground/air strategist, one was a sniper, and one was an expert in hand-to-hand combat. By combining together, they could offer their services to discriminating clients in many countries.
